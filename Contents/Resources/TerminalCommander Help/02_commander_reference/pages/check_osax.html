check_osax

check_osax()

Check installation of TerminalControl.osax and its installed version.

Following errors will raise if there are problems in the installation of TerminalControl.osax.

  • 1701 -- TerminalControl.osax is not installed.
  • 1702 -- the version of TerminalControl.osax is under the required version.
